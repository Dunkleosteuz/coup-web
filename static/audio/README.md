# Audio Assets

## Music Files

### Lobby Music

- **File:** `lobby.mp3`
- **Mood:** Calm, subtle, ambient
- **Purpose:** Plays during lobby/waiting screens
- **Duration:** Loops continuously

### Game Music

- **File:** `game.mp3`
- **Mood:** Energetic, strategic, engaging
- **Purpose:** Plays during active gameplay
- **Duration:** Loops continuously

## Adding Your Own Music

You can replace these files with your own audio files:

1. **Lobby Music:**
   - Keep filename as `lobby.mp3`
   - Recommended: Background ambience, instrumental, calm tempo
   - Format: MP3, WAV, or OGG

2. **Game Music:**
   - Keep filename as `game.mp3`
   - Recommended: Energetic instrumental, upbeat tempo
   - Format: MP3, WAV, or OGG
